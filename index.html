About RepoPulse

RepoPulse is more than just a manager; it's a unified control center designed to simplify the complexity of manage multi-platform and multi-protocol ecosystems.

Built on a high-performance stack using React and Node.js, it prioritizes speed and security. No repository data or Personal Access Tokens are ever transmitted to third-party servers. All interactions are direct from your environment to GitHub/GitLab endpoints.
